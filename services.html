Landscaping Services

A professionally groomed lawn offers multiple benefits - both aesthetic and practical. Not only does lawn care beautify your neighborhood and create feelings of tranquility and privacy, but a healthy lawn will also prevent erosion by trapping/absorbing rainfall and purifying it before it enters the water table. The benefits can also be economical. Proper landscaping and lawn care can positively impact your wallet! The recovery rate of landscape improvements can be well over 100% and well-maintained landscaping can add up to 15% to your home's resale value.

DC Lawn is a full-service lawn and landscape company. We're equipped to implement solutions for all your lawn care and landscape needs. Below, you'll find a few of the services we offer. Please feel free to contact us for any specific landscaping needs you might have. Call (251) 599-2401 for fast, friendly service or to email us for a free quote.
