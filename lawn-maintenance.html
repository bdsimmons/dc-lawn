Lawn Maintenance « Back to Services

We offer weekly and bi-weekly landscape maintenance for Mobile and Baldwin County residents. Our dedicated employees are trained to beautify your lawn in a reliable and affordable manner. A professionally groomed lawn offers multiple benefits - both aesthetic and practical. Not only does lawn care beautify your neighborhood and create feelings of tranquility and privacy, but a healthy lawn will also prevent erosion by trapping/absorbing rainfall and purifying it before it enters the water table. The benefits can also be economical. Proper landscaping and lawn care can positively impact your wallet! The recovery rate of landscape improvements can be well over 100% and well-maintained landscaping can add up to 15% to your home's resale value.

DC Lawn is a full-service lawn and landscape company. We're equipped to implement solutions for all your lawn care and landscape needs. Please feel free to contact us for any specific landscaping needs you might have. Call (251) 599-2401 for fast, friendly service or to email us for a free quote.

  • Retaining walls
